#### Deviations
- **Source Deviations:** Communicate With Care Pocket Guide: "Maintain Professional Tone: Use polite language, avoid sarcasm and humor."

- **Reason:** The new sentence maintains a professional tone and avoids implying that the patient needs empowerment, thereby resolving the potential patronizing deviation.
