### Review and Recommendations

#### 1. **Header Title: "How to talk about treatment options"**

**Guideline Source:**
- Source - EFPIA Chapter 4: Article 21 - Interactions with POs.

**Source Sentence Replacement:**
- Original sentence: "How to talk about treatment options"

**Deviation:**
- According to EFPIA Chapter 4, Article 21.ii: "EU and national laws and regulations prohibit the advertising of POM to the general public."

**New Sentence:**
- "How to discuss your diabetes management options with your healthcare professional"

**Reason for Replacement:**
- The new title avoids any implication of promoting a particular product, ensuring compliance with non-promotional guidelines by focusing on general diabetes management instead of specific treatment options.
